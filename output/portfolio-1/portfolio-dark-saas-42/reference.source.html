About me

Design with
intention & soul

Hello! I'm Mira — a product designer and visual craftsperson based in Amsterdam. Over the past seven years I've worked at the intersection of brand identity, digital product design, and motion, helping startups and scale-ups turn complex ideas into experiences people love.

My process starts with deep listening. Before a single pixel hits the canvas, I spend time understanding your users, your constraints, and the story you want to tell. I believe the best design is both logical and emotional — it solves problems and creates genuine delight.

When I'm not designing, you'll find me shooting 35mm film, sketching typefaces in coffee shops, or obsessing over the kinetic typography in old film title sequences.
