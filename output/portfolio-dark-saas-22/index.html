About Me
My Story

Design is how I make
the world make sense.

I'm Mara Vesper, a product and brand designer based in Amsterdam. Over the past seven years I've helped SaaS startups, creative agencies, and growth-stage companies transform rough ideas into polished digital experiences that users actually love.

My process starts with obsessive research — understanding the people behind the screens before touching a single frame. I merge systematic design thinking with an eye for warmth and craft, because the best interfaces feel both logical and human.

When I'm not pushing pixels, you'll find me sketching in coffee-stained notebooks, hunting for vintage type specimens, or convincing my houseplants to survive another Dutch winter.
